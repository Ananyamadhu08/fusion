Avatar

An avatar is an icon or figure representing a person in a profile section or chat screens etc.
Avatars are available in 4 sizes xs sm md and lg.

Examples

Image Avatars

           
<div class="avatar avatar-lg mr-3">
    <img
      src="/css/images/img_avatar.png"
      alt="avatar-img"
      class="img-round img-responsive"
    />
  </div>
  <div class="avatar avatar-md mr-3">
    <img
      src="/css/images/img_avatar.png"
      alt="avatar-img"
      class="img-round img-responsive"
